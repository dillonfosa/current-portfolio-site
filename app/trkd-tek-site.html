TRKD-TEK

A media consumption concept site design created in 2016 using the following:
HTML5 check
CSS3 check
SKELETON check
PHOTOSHOP CCcheck

DESIGN APPROACH


The idea behind the design

Trkd-Tek is an idea of what a media consumption site. My concept was to have all the user's liked videos, watched Netflix etc. in one dashboard. The site is responsive and works smoothly with Skeleton CSS.

Mobile First Approach

The creation was to allow the user to keep on top of the media. Say they were on the way to thier friends house, or family member's place, they want to use this site on the go. The site is crisp, clear and to the point.
